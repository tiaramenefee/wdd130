Our Story

TareBare Co. started from dream and then later became something real. Our founder, Tiara Menefee always had the joy of creating something out of nothing. As a little girl, she always made jewelry but never thought anything could come of it until she got older. The more she started making jewelry the more she became interested in sharing her creations. All our product is homemade and made with good intentions. Our products include bracelets and necklaces. Each item on the bracelets and necklaces are picked with thoroughness and thought. We find items that are sustainable to the best of our abilities. We promote beauty within. We love the idea of have each one of our products helping journeys to self-love. We want you to be able to wear our product and gain more confidence in yourself. This is why it is important for us to offer the ability to customize your own bracelet or necklace!
