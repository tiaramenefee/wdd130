Home page

Purpose of our site - The purpose of our site is to see our different jewelry that we offer. We have a small section of homemade bracelet and necklace to purchase. It isn’t like normal website for individuals to click on a order now button. We offer the ability to reach out to us through email ( orders@tarebareco.com ). We decided to do this so that everyone can customize their bracelet or necklace. We want individuals to feel like there are apart of the creating process and what better way for this to happen! This allows for each purchase made by the individual to create their beauty within!

Our Story

TareBare Co. started from dream and then later became something real. Our founder, Tiara Menefee always had the joy of creating something out of nothing. As a little girl, she always made jewelry but never thought anything could come of it until she got older. The more she started making jewelry the more she became interested in sharing her creations. All our product is homemade and made with good intentions. Our products include bracelets and necklaces. Each item on the bracelets and necklaces are picked with thoroughness and thought. We find items that are sustainable to the best of our abilities. We promote beauty within. We love the idea of have each one of our products helping journeys to self-love. We want you to be able to wear our product and gain more confidence in yourself. This is why it is important for us to offer the ability to customize your own bracelet or necklace! Our Team TareBare Co. has a great team of two. We call it the dynamic duo. When our founder; Tiara Menefee had the idea of starting the company it was just an idea. She didn’t think it would actually become a reality until her husband Joseph Maddox encouraged her make it a reality. The team consist of a husband and wife. The husband, Joseph Maddox deals with everything financial while Tiara Menefee deals with the creating. They do split other roles in the company such as content writing, emailing, and purchasing.

Products

The process of ordering - You may ask yourself how to I order TareBare Co.’s bracelets and necklace? You have come to the right place for this answer. As you can see, we have a small section to choose from but with each selection is just the base of the bracelet and necklace. With each option, you can customize your bracelet and necklace the way you want! This allows for you (the individual) to feel like you have something unique to you and you are the only one who has the design. Once you have selected the base design of your bracelet and necklace, you can start thinking of idea of how you want to customize your bracelet and necklace. When you have thought about your customizations, you can then email orders@tarebareco.com. Once you have emailed then the discussion of customizations on your bracelet and necklace is talked about. When that is settle then the order is placed by your (individuals) confirmation. If you don’t have customization to make then you can just email your order of what you want! Customizations - You might be asking what do customizations look like? Customizations can be super personal, and we want to be able to make your bracelet or necklace unique to you in every way!
